openmusicvisualizer

This demo visualizes the frequency range in music.

Written in HTML5 and JavaScript. Images are drawn on a canvas element using the RaphaelJS drawing library.

Frequency data obtained from Flash, so it won't work on your precious iDevices.
(Flash 9+ required)

Warning: Please use Chrome. Hundreds of DOM elements are being manipulated at once. Chrome can do this, FF4, Safari and Opera lag, and IE doesn't even try.
